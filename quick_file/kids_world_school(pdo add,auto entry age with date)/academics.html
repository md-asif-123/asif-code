Academics - Subjects Offered

The medium of instruction is English with Sanskrit and Hindi as the Indian languages taught. Great importance is given to the teaching and maintenance of a good standard in all the three languages.

I. The following subjects are taught in Secondary Section Classes VI-X: English, Hindi, Sanskrit (VI to VIII), Mathematics, Science and Technology, Social Studies, Environmental Science, Computer, Art & Craft.

II. The following subjects are taught in Senior Secondary Section Classes XI-XII The Plus-Two section offers three streams-Science, Humanities and Commerce.

Academics - Co-Curricular Activities

Army Public School Kolkata understands the need of the all-round development of the student's personality in making him/her fully equipped to face the world outside School. It is mandatory for all students of Classes V-X to choose one of the following co-curricular clubs that function weekly from the school premises during school hours.

  • Debating and Dramatics
  • Journalism
  • Chinese
  • Nature Club
  • Karate
  • Quiz
  • French
  • Yoga
  • Roller-Skating
  • Music
  • Dance
  • Art and Craft
  • Tabla

The school also has an Audio-Visual Room equipped with Projector, Video camera, Players and Computers.
